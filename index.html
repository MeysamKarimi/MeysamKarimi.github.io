About Me

Summary

In around 2003, Meysam realized that he could automate tasks by writing a small program in C#. His first application, a warehousing application based on the .net programming, inspired him to continue learning how to use C#. Shortly after he graduated in 2006 from university, Meysam became a full-time software developer in Nafta technology development company. it was a learning experience and a true dream job in web application developing using asp.net – one he’s loved doing ever since.

Meysam has been a software developer for more than ten years. Most recently he worked on the Papyrus project, as a development team leader and developer, which is a digital repository under .Net technology (desktop, web and mobile application). Now, he is a full time developer in Solico Group and runs his own consulting group in Tehran, Iran. Meysam is member of Model Driven Software Engineering Research Group in university of Isfahan. He also teaches some courses in Islamic Azad University of Islamshahr, and frequent lecturer at training courses like c#, asp.net and linux.
